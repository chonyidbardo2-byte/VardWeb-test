// legal & transparency

Privacy
Policy

We collect only what we need, protect everything you give us, and never play games with your data. Here is exactly what that means.

Last updated: April 2026

// 01 — who we are

Built for the web. Accountable to you.

VardWeb is a Canadian web design and development studio — homegrown and proudly so — that builds precision-crafted digital experiences for businesses that take their online presence seriously. We operate at vardweb.ca and vardweb.com, both of which are subject to this Privacy Policy. The same exacting standards we bring to every website we build apply equally to how we handle your data.

// 02 — what we collect & why

Only what's necessary. Nothing more.

We do not collect data for sport. Every piece of information we receive serves a specific, transparent purpose.

Contact Forms

When you submit a form on vardweb.ca or vardweb.com, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry and follow up on your request. It is never sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party — for any reason.

Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies to keep the site running correctly:

  • Session cookies — created when you interact with the site and discarded the moment you close your browser. These carry no personal data.
  • Preference cookies (opt-in only) — if you choose to save your details for convenience, these persist for up to one year and can be cleared at any time.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregated data about how visitors interact with vardweb.ca and vardweb.com. This helps us identify what is working and where we can improve. No personally identifiable information is tied to analytics data. Google processes this data under their own Privacy Policy.

Media Uploads

If you upload images to the site, ensure they do not contain embedded location data (EXIF GPS). Uploaded images are publicly accessible and their metadata can be extracted by anyone who downloads them.

Embedded Content

Some pages may include embedded content from third-party platforms — videos, maps, or previews. This content behaves as if you visited those platforms directly and may collect data or use cookies under their own independent privacy policies.

// 03 — data sharing

We don't share. Full stop.

🔒

Your personal data is never sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party. The only data that leaves our control is anonymized analytics, processed by Google Analytics under their own terms — and even that cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

// 04 — data retention

We hold what we need, no longer.

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to address your request. Anonymized analytics data follows Google Analytics' default retention policy. You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time — see your rights below.

// 05 — your rights

Your data. Your call.

Regardless of where you are in the world, you have meaningful rights over your personal information. We honour them without friction.

📋
AccessRequest a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
✏️
CorrectionRequest that inaccurate information be updated.
🗑️
ErasureRequest deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
↩️
WithdrawalWithdraw any consent you have given, at any time.

// 06 — reach out

Questions? We're a message away.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or the personal data we hold, don't hesitate to contact us directly. We respond promptly.
