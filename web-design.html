// how it works

From idea to live site
in four steps

01 — Discovery Call

We dig into your goals, audience, and brand. 30 minutes, no jargon. You walk away with a clear scope and a fixed quote.

02 — Wireframe & Concept

We map out page structure and layout before touching visual design. You approve the architecture before a single pixel is pushed.

03 — Design & Build

Your site is designed and built in-browser — what you see is what ships. We send a live preview link for review and feedback.

04 — QA, Launch & Handoff

Cross-browser testing, speed checks, and accessibility audit before we go live. Full source code handed over on day one post-launch.
